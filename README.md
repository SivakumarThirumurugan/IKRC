### 🏎️ Project Overview
This repository showcases the mechanical design and engineering management involved in developing a high-performance racing kart for the Indian Kart Racing Championship (IKRC), hosted by Titan Autocorp[cite: 1]. As the Design Team Leader[cite: 1], I directed the end-to-end mechanical engineering lifecycle, translating conceptual track requirements into a manufacturable, competition-ready vehicle.

### ⚙️ Core Responsibilities & Contributions
*   **Engineering Leadership:** Directed the mechanical design team[cite: 1] through the complete product development cycle, ensuring adherence to strict IKRC technical and safety regulations.
*   **Chassis & Systems Design:** Executed the primary mechanical design[cite: 1] of the racing kart, focusing on structural integrity, driver ergonomics, and weight optimization.
*   **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Coordinated between fabrication, assembly, and testing units to ensure the CAD models translated accurately into physical prototypes.
*   **Design Validation:** Monitored mechanical feasibility and manufacturing tolerances to withstand the dynamic loads of competitive kart racing.
